VA Disability Insight
A browser extension that enhances the VA.gov disability ratings page with additional information including diagnostic codes, full condition names, static/non-static status, and more — built by a veteran, for veterans.
Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 14, 2026
Overview
VA Disability Insight is a browser extension that enhances the VA.gov disability ratings page by displaying additional information about your service-connected disability ratings.
Data Collection
VA Disability Insight does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data of any kind. The extension reads information already displayed on your VA.gov ratings page and presents it in an enhanced format within your browser. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.
Permissions
The extension requires access to VA.gov solely to read and enhance the disability ratings page. This permission is used only to display additional information to you and for no other purpose.
Third Parties
This extension does not share any data with third parties. There are no third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising of any kind included in this extension.
Contact
For questions or support, visit the extension listing on the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons page.
